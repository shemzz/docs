## **Overview**

Voice configuration allows you to customize how your Mavera AI agent sounds during conversations. You can select voices, adjust parameters, and fine-tune the speaking style to match your brand.

![Voice Configuration Overview](/images/voice-configuration-overview-placeholder.png)

## **Voice Selection**

### **Available Voices**

Mavera AI offers a variety of voices:
- **Gender Options**: Male, Female, Neutral
- **Accents**: US, UK, Australian, Canadian, and more
- **Languages**: Multiple language support
- **Styles**: Professional, Friendly, Casual, etc.

![Voice Selection Interface](/images/voice-selection-interface-placeholder.png)

### **Choosing the Right Voice**

Consider these factors:
- **Brand Identity**: Does the voice match your brand?
- **Target Audience**: What voice will resonate with your customers?
- **Use Case**: Different voices work better for different scenarios
- **Language**: Match voice language with conversation language

## **Basic Voice Settings**

### **Voice Parameters**

Configure basic voice characteristics:

- **Voice**: Select from available voices
- **Gender**: Male, Female, or Neutral
- **Accent**: Regional accent selection
- **Language**: Primary language

![Basic Voice Settings](/images/basic-voice-settings-placeholder.png)

### **Speaking Rate**

Control how fast your agent speaks:
- **Slow**: More deliberate, easier to understand
- **Normal**: Standard speaking pace
- **Fast**: Quicker delivery

**Recommendation**: Start with Normal and adjust based on testing.

## **Advanced Voice Settings**

### **Pitch Control**

Adjust voice pitch:
- **Lower Pitch**: Deeper, more authoritative
- **Normal Pitch**: Standard pitch
- **Higher Pitch**: Lighter, more energetic

### **Volume Control**

Adjust voice volume:
- **Quiet**: Lower volume
- **Normal**: Standard volume
- **Loud**: Higher volume

### **Pause Duration**

Control pauses between sentences:
- **Short Pauses**: Faster delivery
- **Normal Pauses**: Standard timing
- **Long Pauses**: More deliberate pacing

![Advanced Voice Settings](/images/advanced-voice-settings-placeholder.png)

## **Voice Styles**

### **Professional**

Formal, business-appropriate tone:
- Clear articulation
- Moderate pace
- Professional tone
- Suitable for: Business calls, B2B interactions

### **Friendly**

Warm, approachable tone:
- Conversational pace
- Warm tone
- Engaging delivery
- Suitable for: Customer service, support

### **Casual**

Relaxed, informal tone:
- Natural pace
- Conversational style
- Less formal
- Suitable for: Informal interactions, consumer-facing

### **Energetic**

High-energy, enthusiastic tone:
- Faster pace
- Upbeat delivery
- Excited tone
- Suitable for: Sales, marketing, promotions

## **Voice Testing**

### **Preview Voice**

Before deploying:
1. Select your voice configuration
2. Click **"Preview"** or **"Test Voice"**
3. Listen to sample phrases
4. Adjust settings as needed

![Voice Preview](/images/voice-preview-placeholder.png)

### **Test in Context**

Test voice in actual conversations:
1. Use simulation feature
2. Make test calls
3. Listen to full conversations
4. Adjust based on feedback

## **Best Practices**

### **1. Match Voice to Use Case**

Choose voice appropriate for your scenario:
- Customer service: Friendly, clear
- Sales: Energetic, persuasive
- Support: Professional, helpful
- Information: Clear, neutral

### **2. Consider Your Audience**

Think about your target audience:
- Age group
- Cultural preferences
- Regional preferences
- Language requirements

### **3. Test Thoroughly**

Always test voice settings:
- Test with real users
- Get feedback
- Adjust based on results
- Monitor performance

### **4. Keep It Natural**

Avoid over-tuning:
- Don't make voice too fast or slow
- Maintain natural pauses
- Keep pitch in normal range
- Ensure clarity

### **5. Update Regularly**

Review and update voice settings:
- Monitor user feedback
- Test new voices
- Adjust based on performance
- Stay current with options

## **Voice Quality Tips**

### **Clarity**

Ensure clear speech:
- Use appropriate speaking rate
- Avoid too-fast delivery
- Ensure proper pauses
- Test with different accents

### **Naturalness**

Make voice sound natural:
- Use appropriate pauses
- Vary speaking rate slightly
- Match tone to content
- Avoid robotic delivery

### **Consistency**

Maintain consistent voice:
- Use same voice across agents
- Keep settings consistent
- Standardize configuration
- Document voice choices

## **Common Issues**

### **Voice Too Fast**

**Solution:**
- Reduce speaking rate
- Increase pause duration
- Test with users
- Adjust gradually

### **Voice Too Slow**

**Solution:**
- Increase speaking rate
- Reduce pause duration
- Test with users
- Find optimal balance

### **Voice Unclear**

**Solution:**
- Check audio quality
- Adjust speaking rate
- Test different voices
- Verify audio settings

### **Voice Doesn't Match Brand**

**Solution:**
- Try different voice options
- Adjust style settings
- Test multiple voices
- Get stakeholder feedback
